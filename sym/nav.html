Upagraha '22

The department of ECE of Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering is proud to launch Its National level Tech Symposium, with the prime focus of encouraging young tech enthusiasts to engage in vigorous research and take engineering to a whole new level. Upagraha '22 will act as a platform to exchange ideas and scientific knowledge through intense professional interaction. We welcome you to participate and highlight your talent in front of eminent professors, industrial professionals, and talented students in a formal environment.

DAY 2

MAZE BOT

Bring out your best bot!!

MAZE BOT

It’s a time fraught with tension for the Avengers, charged with bringing the Tesseract, containing the Space Stone, one of the six Infinity Stones home to safety. The stone’s immense power has attracted hordes of villains and power-hungry beings around the universe, who are lying in ambush. It is your job to map out a safe and secure path for Tony Stark to get home with the stone. His life, and the fate of the entire universe, is in your hands! It’s time to electrify, following lines on the floor. Get ready for the “Maze Bot “event. A fully autonomous line-follower robot will be put into an ultimate test. The robots must navigate a maze and beat the clock to win. Set your robot, program it, complete the task, and win the prize!

Rules

• Participants must bring a completely autonomous line-follower robot. The robot should not have features with which it can be controlled remotely.
• A team consisting of a maximum of 2 people can participate. (Registration to be done individually)
• Participants must only use standard BO motors (not more than 100 rpm) in their robot for movement.
• If and whenever the robot wanders off the path, it'll be reset to its last position on the path and three seconds will be added to the total time.
• A robot is considered to have finished the course only when the forward most wheel completely crosses the finish line.
• Sensors and output devices present in the robot which are beyond the scope of a line follower will not be looked upon favorably by the moderators.
• All robots will be thoroughly inspected by the moderators before the challenge begins.
• The line-follower robot which completes the course in the least amount of time provided it accurately follows the given path will be declared the winner.
• The decision of the moderators regarding the rules and the conduct of the event shall be final and binding.
Register here!
